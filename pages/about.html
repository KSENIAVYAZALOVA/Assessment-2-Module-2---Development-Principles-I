About Dragoncove 3D

Dragon's Cove 3D came around with an idea and a passion for new technology. As a passionate Digital Technology teacher, Daniel Wyatt creates 2D forms that come to the 3D world and shows people from all walks of life that new technology is fun and functional to learn, as well as easy to create new items from scratch. By combining the two, Daniel Wyatt has created a business that allows people to see and feel how these items are created and allows people to express their imagination through new ways of using technology.
